# Fluenta

Welcome to **Fluenta**, an intelligent and dynamic language learning platform designed to revolutionize the way individuals master English. By moving away from one-size-fits-all curricula, Fluenta adapts to every learner's unique goals, native language, and current proficiency to deliver a truly personalized educational journey.

## The Vision

Language learning should be engaging, relevant, and fluid. Fluenta was built on the philosophy that students learn best when they are challenged at precisely their level and when the content reflects their real-world objectives—whether that's thriving in a business environment, preparing for academic excellence, or traveling the globe.

## Core Features & Experience

### 🎯 Adaptive Placement & CEFR Alignment
Fluenta strictly adheres to the internationally recognized Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), guiding learners from absolute beginner (A1) to advanced mastery (C1). The journey begins with an adaptive Placement Test that evaluates a user's reading, grammar, and vocabulary skills, ensuring they start at the perfect level.

### 🧠 Intelligent Curriculum Generation
Instead of a static list of lessons, Fluenta crafts a dynamic curriculum. Based on the user's proficiency and goals, the platform generates tailored modules and lessons. Each module is structured to build foundational skills first, gradually increasing in complexity as the learner demonstrates mastery.

### 📚 Comprehensive Skill Development
Fluenta doesn't just focus on multiple-choice questions; it provides a holistic approach to language acquisition through four core pillars:
- **Listening:** Engaging with curated, educational video content and interactive transcripts, followed by active recall and fill-in-the-blank exercises.
- **Reading:** Analyzing leveled texts and answering comprehension questions to build vocabulary and contextual understanding.
- **Writing:** Open-ended writing prompts where users receive real-time, constructive feedback on their grammar, style, and vocabulary usage.
- **Speaking:** Audio-based prompts that allow users to practice pronunciation and conversational flow, with intelligent transcription and scoring.

### 🤖 The Socratic AI Tutor (Lex)
Within every lesson, users have access to **Lex**, an integrated, intelligent tutor. Lex is uniquely designed with a Socratic approach: rather than giving away direct answers, Lex asks guiding questions, provides hints, and encourages learners to arrive at the correct conclusions on their own. This promotes deep cognitive engagement and better long-term retention.

### 📈 Mastery and Progression
Learning on Fluenta is gamified yet rigorous. Lessons and modules are locked until the user proves their competence. At the end of every lesson, an assessment calculates a mastery score across all skills. Users build daily streaks and can track their moving averages in Reading, Writing, Listening, and Speaking over time.

### 💬 Community & Peer Interaction
Language is inherently social. Fluenta features a built-in community hub where learners can join public rooms (e.g., "Grammar Help", "Free Talk", "Speaking Practice") to converse with peers worldwide. It also supports direct messaging, allowing users to form study partnerships and practice conversational English in a safe, collaborative environment.

## The Learning Journey

1. **Onboarding & Personalization:** Tell Fluenta your native language and primary goal.
2. **Assessment:** Take the adaptive test to establish your CEFR baseline.
3. **The Roadmap:** Follow a clear, module-based path tailored to your needs.
4. **Active Learning:** Watch, read, write, and speak your way through engaging lessons.
5. **Mastery:** Pass assessments, unlock new content, and watch your proficiency soar.
